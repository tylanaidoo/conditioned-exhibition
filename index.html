Conditioned

This project begins with a simple premise: if you could walk through a gallery that showed you exactly what AI has done to human culture, what would you see? This exhibition builds that gallery, where every room is painted from real big data.

Tyla Naidoo  ·  MSc Big Data in Culture and Society
King’s College London  ·  2025–26
